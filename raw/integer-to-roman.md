Integer to Roman
===


Problem
-------

Given an integer, convert it to a roman numeral.

The number is guaranteed to be within the range from 1 to 3999.

Example
-------

4 -> IV

12 -> XII

21 -> XXI

99 -> XCIX

more examples at: http://literacy.kent.edu/Minigrants/Cinci/romanchart.htm
